EXEMPLARY AID

Having observed the difficulties some elderly people face in fulfilling their daily chores, I set out to come up with a plan to assist those less able to make some of their tasks easier, this can be done by taking their stress out of the equation as they won't have to rely on friends, family or professional.

ACCOMPANIMENT FOR SHOPPING

I can offer accompanied assistance on shopping trips, whether it's in a Supermarket, a shop on the High Street, in a Garden or Shopping Centre.

This could involve:
  • In-person or virtual shopping support helps you choose items or carry out returns.
  • Assistance with the shopping bags/trolley or queuing at the tills.
    • These are some of the ways I can help enhance your shopping experience by making it more convenient and enjoyable.

    WALKING DOGS

    Being a dog career for my own Labrador over the last 7 years, I have grown fond of animals and can care for them.

    I can help with dog walking (accompanied or alone) as well as dog sitting during my spare time, this not only helps maintain the dog's health and happiness but also eases the physical burden on the seniors allowing them to enjoy the companionship of their pets without worrying about strenuous or regular walks.

    RUNNING NECESSARY ERRANDS

    I can run your essential errands by picking up groceries, dry cleaning collecting, medical prescriptions with the GP
    (subject to authorisation), or handling admin tasks such as typing, posting letters or cards.
